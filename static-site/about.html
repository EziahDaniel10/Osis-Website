About OSIS

One Spirit. One Mission. One Nigeria.

Who We Are

Nigeria's Gateway to Electric Mobility

One Spirit Integrated Services Limited (OSIS) was established with a singular purpose: to accelerate Nigeria's transition to clean, sustainable transportation. As the exclusive distributor of the BNC Challenger S110 electric motorcycle in Nigeria, we are at the forefront of an energy revolution that is transforming the way Nigerians move.

We saw the opportunity — a country hungry for reliable, affordable, and eco-friendly transportation — and we answered it. By partnering with BNC, one of the world's leading electric motorcycle manufacturers, we brought the Challenger S110 to Nigerian roads, giving riders a machine engineered for performance, built for durability, and designed with the future in mind.

OSIS is more than a distributor. We are a movement. Every bike we sell is a step toward cleaner air, lower transportation costs, and a more connected Nigeria.
